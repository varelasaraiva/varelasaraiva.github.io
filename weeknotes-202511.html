  • There was today the first Formula 1 Grand Prix of the season in Melbourne. I’m watching it as I write this week’s notes. It is being quite entertaining. The McLaren seems to be the class of the field, confirming the suspicions of the pre-season testing. It seems this year’s car is very good at not putting too much strain in the tires, slowing the rate of decay in performance and allowing the drivers to push for longer.
